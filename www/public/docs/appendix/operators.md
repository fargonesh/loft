# Operator Reference

## Arithmetic
- `+` Addition
- `-` Subtraction
- `*` Multiplication
- `/` Division
- `%` Modulo

## Comparison
- `==` Equal
- `!=` Not equal
- `<` Less than
- `>` Greater than
- `<=` Less than or equal
- `>=` Greater than or equal

## Logical
- `&&` And
- `||` Or
- `!` Not

## Assignment
- `=` Assignment

## Other
- `?` Error propagation
- `.` Member access
- `[]` Index access
